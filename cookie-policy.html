Cookies Policy

Effective Date: 12 May 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Accendi Futuro (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience on https://accendifuturo.org/.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser when you visit websites. They allow websites to recognize your browser and store certain information such as preferences or login status.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use only essential and analytical cookies. These include:

  • Analytical cookies (e.g., Google Analytics): To understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Stored on your device for a set period (ours expire after 30 days).

3. No Advertising Cookies

We do not use cookies for advertising or marketing purposes. No personal information is tracked or shared.

4. Cookie Duration

All cookies used by our site automatically expire after 30 days or less.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to:

  • Accept or reject cookies by default
  • Delete stored cookies
  • Notify you when cookies are being set

Note: Blocking cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may revise this Cookies Policy as necessary. Please check back periodically for any updates.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, you can contact us at: info@accendifuturo.org
