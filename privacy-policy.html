Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12 May 2025

Accendi Futuro (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website https://accendifuturo.org/, how we use that information, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers from users visiting our website.

2. Traffic Analytics

We use third-party tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand website usage patterns and improve user experience. These tools collect anonymous information such as:

  • Device and browser type
  • IP address (used in anonymized form)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source (e.g., Google search)

This data is collected strictly for analytical purposes and cannot be used to personally identify any user.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to gather limited usage data. Cookies are small text files placed on your device. For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.

All cookies we use expire automatically after 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties for marketing or profiling purposes. Any third-party services used (such as analytics tools) are chosen for their GDPR-compliant practices.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data, we take standard security measures to protect our website and any backend services from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Your Rights

Although we do not process personal data, we respect the digital rights of our visitors. If you have any questions about your data privacy, you can contact us.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: info@accendifuturo.org
