About Accendi il Futuro

Catania, at the heart of the Mediterranean, holds incredible potential but it also faces high youth unemployment and limited access to digital and entrepreneurial skills. Accendi il Futuro was created to bridge that gap and build an inclusive innovation ecosystem connecting local talent to global opportunities.

  • Advanced training in coding, AI, robotics, and cybersecurity
  • Startup support with mentoring, funding, and global connections
  • Leadership and social innovation programs for young changemakers

Our Roots & Model

Inspired by South LA

Accendi il Futuro is directly inspired by and connected to the experience of the South LA Best Buy Teen Tech Center, a project founded by Cassie Betts, the same visionary mind behind this new initiative in Sicily.

In South Los Angeles, an area marked by inequality and lack of opportunity, the Teen Tech Center became a driver of transformation, offering tech training, startup incubation, and access to international networks. This model has enabled hundreds of young people to gain skills in emerging sectors and access careers in technology and innovation.

Why Catania & Sicily?

Why Sicily? Because today more than ever, the island is called to play a key role in the Mediterranean:

  • Strategic position: A crossroads between Europe, Africa, and Asia, and a reference point for new trade and technology routes.
  • Academic and scientific excellence: The University of Catania and local research centers are essential resources for developing an innovation ecosystem.
  • Emerging industries: Sectors such as aerospace, artificial intelligence, and renewable energy are growing rapidly but need qualified human capital.
